Asset Types & Currency Conversion

CEDEAR — Argentine Depositary Receipt. Local wrapper for US stocks, priced in ARS on BYMA. USD value derived via MEP rate. The ratio (e.g., 1:20) shows how many CEDEARs equal one US share.

Argentine Stock — Equity listed on BYMA, priced in ARS. USD value derived via MEP rate.

US Stock — Equity listed on NYSE/NASDAQ, priced in USD. No currency conversion needed.

MEP (Dolar MEP) — Implicit USD exchange rate obtained via local bond pair trades (AL30/AL30D). Used to convert ARS-priced assets to USD. Source: Data912.

CCL (Dolar CCL) — Implicit USD rate via ADR arbitrage (settles abroad). Used to display US stocks in ARS.

Portfolio

Avg ARS — Average purchase price in ARS per share. Only shown for Argentine assets (CEDEARs, Arg stocks).

Avg USD — Average purchase price in USD per share.
Argentine assets: avg_price_ars ÷ entry_mep_rate
US stocks: the purchase price itself (already in USD).

Price USD — Current market price in USD.
Argentine assets: current_price_ars ÷ current_mep_rate
US stocks: the market price itself.

Price % — Price change since purchase in the asset's native currency.
(current_price − avg_price) ÷ avg_price × 100

Currency % — Change in the MEP rate since purchase, reflecting peso depreciation or appreciation. Only applies to Argentine assets.
(entry_mep_rate ÷ current_mep_rate − 1) × 100

USD Ret % — Total return in USD, combining price and currency effects. Compounding avoids overstating or understating the real USD result.
(1 + price%) × (1 + currency%) − 1

Value USD — Total position value in USD.
shares × price_usd

USD P&L — Profit or loss in US dollars.
(price_usd − avg_usd) × shares

Weight % — Position size as a percentage of total portfolio value.
value_usd ÷ total_portfolio_usd × 100

Technicals

Colors show portfolio health: green = holding is strong, red = holding is struggling. Colors fade through white — only extremes get full color.

% Below ATH — How far the current price is below its all-time high.
Full green at 0% (at ATH), fades to white at 15%, full red at 30%+.
(price ÷ all_time_high − 1) × 100

RSI(14) — Relative Strength Index over 14 periods. Measures momentum by comparing average gains vs average losses.
Full red ≤20 (deeply oversold), fades to white at 30, white 30–70 (neutral), fades to green at 80, full green ≥80 (strong momentum).
100 − 100 ÷ (1 + avg_gain ÷ avg_loss)

Stoch K/D — Stochastic oscillator. %K = where price closed vs the high-low range (14 periods). %D = 3-period SMA of %K. When K crosses above D, momentum is shifting up (and vice versa).
Full red at 0 (deeply oversold), fades to white at 20, white 20–80 (neutral), fades to green at 80, full green at 100.
%K = (close − low₁₄) ÷ (high₁₄ − low₁₄) × 100

Supertrend — Trend-following indicator based on Average True Range (ATR).
Green = uptrend (price above band), red = downtrend (price below band).
upper = (high + low) ÷ 2 + multiplier × ATR

vs SMA20 / vs SMA50 — How far the price is above or below the 20/50-day simple moving average, shown as %.
White within ±1%, fades to full green at +10% (above SMA) or full red at −10% (below SMA). Tooltip shows actual SMA value. For CEDEARs, uses the underlying US stock price for comparison.
(price − SMA) ÷ SMA × 100

Health — Combined score from 5 indicators. Each contributes +1 (bullish) or −1 (bearish):
RSI ≥50 = +1, Stoch K > D = +1, Supertrend up = +1, price > SMA20 = +1, price > SMA50 = +1.
Range: +5 (all bullish) to −5 (all bearish). Hover for per-indicator breakdown.

Fundamentals

Metrics with color are compared to sector medians: green = better than sector, yellow = near sector average, red = worse than sector. When no sector data is available, absolute thresholds are used.

P/E — Price to trailing twelve-month earnings. Lower is better (cheaper valuation). Green below sector median, red above 1.5×.
share_price ÷ earnings_per_share

Fwd P/E — Price to estimated forward earnings. Same interpretation as P/E but based on analyst estimates.
share_price ÷ estimated_future_eps

P/B — Price to book value per share. Compares market price to accounting value.
share_price ÷ book_value_per_share

ROE % — Return on equity. Higher is better (efficient use of capital). Green above sector median, red below 50% of it.
net_income ÷ shareholder_equity × 100

EPS Gr % — Year-over-year earnings per share growth.
(eps_current − eps_prior) ÷ |eps_prior| × 100

Div Yld % — Annual dividend yield as a percentage of share price.
annual_dividends ÷ share_price × 100

D/E — Debt to equity ratio. Lower is better (less leverage). Green below sector median, red above 2×.
total_debt ÷ shareholder_equity

Margin % — Net profit margin. Higher is better. Green above sector median, red below 50% of it.
net_income ÷ revenue × 100
