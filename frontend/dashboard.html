Asset Types & Currency Conversion

How each asset type is priced and converted between ARS and USD.

CEDEAR
Argentine Depositary Receipt
Local wrapper for US stocks, priced in ARS on BYMA. USD value derived via MEP rate. The ratio (e.g., 1:20) shows how many CEDEARs equal one US share.
Argentine Stock
BYMA-listed equity
Priced in ARS. USD value derived via MEP rate.
US Stock
NYSE / NASDAQ equity
Priced in USD. No currency conversion needed.
MEP (Dolar MEP)
Implicit USD rate via local bond trades
Obtained via local bond pair trades (AL30/AL30D). Used to convert ARS-priced assets to USD. Source: Data912.
CCL (Dolar CCL)
Implicit USD rate via ADR arbitrage
Settles abroad. Used to display US stocks in ARS.

Portfolio

Columns shown in the Portfolio tab and how each value is calculated.

Avg ARS
Average purchase price in pesos
Per-share average cost in ARS. Only shown for Argentine assets (CEDEARs, Arg stocks).
Avg USD
Average purchase price in dollars
Argentine assets: avg_price_ars ÷ entry_mep_rate
US stocks: the purchase price itself (already in USD).
Price USD
Current market price in dollars
Argentine assets: current_price_ars ÷ current_mep_rate
US stocks: the market price itself.
Price %
Price change since purchase in native currency
(current_price − avg_price) ÷ avg_price × 100
Currency %
MEP rate change since purchase
Reflects peso depreciation or appreciation. Only applies to Argentine assets.
(entry_mep_rate ÷ current_mep_rate − 1) × 100
USD Ret %
Total compounded return in USD
Combines price and currency effects. Compounding avoids overstating or understating the real USD result.
(1 + price%) × (1 + currency%) − 1
Value USD
Total position value in dollars
shares × price_usd
USD P&L
Profit or loss in dollars
(price_usd − avg_usd) × shares
Weight %
Position size as portfolio percentage
value_usd ÷ total_portfolio_usd × 100

Technicals

Colors show portfolio health: green = holding is strong, red = holding is struggling. Colors fade through white — only extremes get full color.

% Below ATH
Distance from all-time high
Full green at 0% (at ATH), fades to white at 15%, full red at 30%+.
(price ÷ all_time_high − 1) × 100
RSI(14)
Relative Strength Index — momentum oscillator
Compares average gains vs average losses over 14 periods.
Full red ≤20 (deeply oversold), fades to white at 30, white 30–70 (neutral), fades to green at 80, full green ≥80 (strong momentum).
100 − 100 ÷ (1 + avg_gain ÷ avg_loss)
Stoch K/D
Stochastic oscillator — overbought / oversold
%K = where price closed vs the high-low range (14 periods). %D = 3-period SMA of %K. When K crosses above D, momentum is shifting up (and vice versa).
Full red at 0 (deeply oversold), fades to white at 20, white 20–80 (neutral), fades to green at 80, full green at 100.
%K = (close − low₁₄) ÷ (high₁₄ − low₁₄) × 100
Supertrend
Trend-following indicator based on ATR
Green = uptrend (price above band), red = downtrend (price below band).
upper = (high + low) ÷ 2 + multiplier × ATR
vs SMA20 / vs SMA50
Distance from moving averages
How far the price is above or below the 20/50-day simple moving average, shown as %.
White within ±1%, fades to full green at +10% or full red at −10%. Tooltip shows actual SMA value. For CEDEARs, uses the underlying US stock price.
(price − SMA) ÷ SMA × 100
Health
Composite technical score (−5 to +5)
Each of 5 indicators contributes +1 (bullish) or −1 (bearish):
RSI ≥50, Stoch K > D, Supertrend up, price > SMA20, price > SMA50.
Range: +5 (all bullish) to −5 (all bearish). Hover for breakdown.

Fundamentals

Metrics compared to sector medians: green = better than sector, yellow = near average, red = worse. When no sector data is available, absolute thresholds are used.

P/E
Price to trailing earnings — valuation
Lower is better (cheaper). Green below sector median, red above 1.5×.
share_price ÷ earnings_per_share
Fwd P/E
Price to estimated forward earnings
Same as P/E but based on analyst estimates.
share_price ÷ estimated_future_eps
P/B
Price to book value — valuation
Compares market price to accounting (book) value.
share_price ÷ book_value_per_share
ROE %
Return on equity — profitability
Higher is better (efficient use of capital). Green above sector median, red below 50% of it.
net_income ÷ shareholder_equity × 100
EPS Gr %
Earnings per share growth — year over year
(eps_current − eps_prior) ÷ |eps_prior| × 100
Div Yld %
Annual dividend yield
annual_dividends ÷ share_price × 100
D/E
Debt to equity — leverage
Lower is better (less leverage). Green below sector median, red above 2×.
total_debt ÷ shareholder_equity
Margin %
Net profit margin — profitability
Higher is better. Green above sector median, red below 50% of it.
net_income ÷ revenue × 100
